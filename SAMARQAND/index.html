About Us

SAMARQAND is a premier distributor and wholesaler of high-quality candies and chocolates, renowned for its commitment to excellence in the global sweets market. Specializing in both import and export services, we connect international suppliers and local retailers, delivering a curated selection of premium confections from around the world. With years of experience in the industry, SAMARQAND has built a strong reputation for reliability, efficiency, and exceptional customer service. Our global sourcing network allows us to offer an extensive portfolio of renowned brands and artisanal treats, ensuring that our clients receive only the finest products tailored to their market needs. Whether you operate a supermarket chain, boutique candy shop, or large-scale distribution business, SAMARQAND is your trusted partner for seamless logistics, competitive pricing, and sweets that delight.
